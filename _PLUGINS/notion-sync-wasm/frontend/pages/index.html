Setup Required
  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations and create a new internal integration
  2. Copy the Internal Integration Token
  3. Open Studio Settings > Plugins > Notion Sync and paste the token
  4. In Notion, open your database and click ... > Connections > Add your integration
  5. Return here and select your database below
