# Independent Courier

A starter example job. Replace or adapt this for your world.

Independent couriers operate in the space between official delivery services and outright criminal work. They know every route, every checkpoint, every guard whose attention can be diverted. The job pays modestly but offers freedom and information — two things worth more than a steady wage in this district.

## Skills Required

- Geographic knowledge of the district
- Discretion
- Physical fitness
- Ability to read situations quickly

## Social Position

Low official status, high practical value. Couriers are treated as invisible by the powerful and essential by everyone else.
