# Dr. Vera Okonkwo

A starter example mentor character. Adapt this to fit your world's power structure.

Vera spent thirty years building a reputation as the city's most reliable information broker disguised as an academic. She knows where every body is buried — figuratively and occasionally literally.

## Personality

Sharp, methodical, with a wry warmth she deploys strategically. Genuinely invested in younger people who show potential, but allergic to naivety.

## Goals

- Protect her network at all costs
- Ensure the knowledge she's accumulated doesn't die with her
- Keep her past from catching up with her
