# The Old Quarter

A starter example district. Replace or adapt this for your world.

The Old Quarter is the city's oldest neighbourhood — a dense grid of pre-war buildings that survived every wave of redevelopment through a combination of heritage protections and organised resistance. It is simultaneously the poorest and most culturally rich district in the city.

## Character

Contradictions define the Old Quarter: poverty and creativity, danger and community, history and decay. Streets here have names that predate the current regime. People keep records of things the official histories have forgotten.
