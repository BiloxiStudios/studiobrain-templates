# Gear Head Tools Brand Sheet

## Brand Overview
Gear Head Tools represents honest, blue-collar American manufacturing in the City of Brains universe. Founded by Rusty McDaniels in 1983, this small but respected tool company has built a reputation for creating durable, reliable automotive tools that professional mechanics can depend on.

## Corporate History

### Founding Story
Rusty McDaniels started Gear Head Tools after years of frustration with cheap, imported tools that broke during critical repairs. Working out of his garage, he began forging his own wrenches and selling them to fellow mechanics. Word spread quickly about the quality and durability of his handmade tools.

### Growth Timeline
- **1983**: Founded by Rusty McDaniels in his garage
- **1985**: First retail partnership with local auto parts stores
- **1987**: Expanded to small manufacturing facility
- **1991**: Won "Best Professional Tools" award at Regional Trade Show
- **1995**: Opened second location in Southside
- **1998**: Launched hydraulic equipment line
- **2003**: Third location in Industrial District

### Corporate Evolution
The company has maintained its commitment to American manufacturing and quality craftsmanship despite pressure to outsource production. Rusty's insistence on testing every tool personally has become both a selling point and a business limitation.

### Current Status
Pre-outbreak, Gear Head Tools was a small but profitable regional brand with strong customer loyalty among professional mechanics. The company employed 23 people and had consistent year-over-year growth.

## Brand Identity Deep Dive

### Visual Language
The Gear Head Tools aesthetic draws heavily from American industrial design of the 1940s-50s. Bold, angular typography suggests strength and reliability, while the rust orange color palette evokes both the patina of well-used tools and the warmth of a working garage.

### Voice and Tone
Gear Head Tools communicates like an experienced mechanic: direct, honest, and unpretentious. The brand avoids technical jargon in favor of plain language that emphasizes practical benefits. "This wrench works" carries more weight than complex specifications.

### Brand Positioning
Positioned as the choice of "real mechanics," Gear Head Tools differentiates itself from mass-market tool brands through quality and from premium brands through affordability. The brand occupies the sweet spot between professional quality and working-class accessibility.

### Brand Promise
"Tools That Work As Hard As You Do" - Gear Head Tools promises reliability under the toughest conditions. Every tool comes with Rusty's personal guarantee: if it breaks during normal use, he'll replace it personally.

## Marketing and Advertising

### Brand Campaigns
The most successful campaign featured real customers talking about how Gear Head Tools helped them through difficult repairs. "Mechanics' Stories" ran for three years and significantly boosted brand recognition.

### Advertising Strategy
Gear Head Tools advertises primarily through trade publications, local radio during drive-time hours, and sponsorship of automotive events. The approach is grassroots rather than mass-market.

### Promotional Activities
- Annual "Gear Head Challenge" at local auto shows
- Sponsorship of high school automotive programs
- Free tool maintenance clinics at shops
- "Tool of the Month" program for loyal customers

## Dark Secrets and Conspiracies
Unlike many brands in the City of Brains universe, Gear Head Tools has no dark secrets or conspiracy connections. This genuine honesty makes the brand stand out as one of the few "clean" companies in the region - which may be why it remained small and regional rather than achieving massive growth like more corrupt competitors.

## Post-Outbreak Evolution

### Outbreak Impact
The zombie outbreak effectively ended Gear Head Tools as an operating business. The small manufacturing facility was abandoned when workers fled the city, and the retail locations were either looted or left empty.

### Current Operations
All operations have ceased. The manufacturing equipment remains in the factory, potentially valuable to survivors who need to repair vehicles or create weapons.

### Survivor Relations
Survivors who remember Gear Head Tools speak fondly of the brand and Rusty McDaniels. The tools that were already in circulation have become prized possessions, traded at premium values among survivor communities.

### Legacy and Consequences
Gear Head Tools serves as a symbol of what was lost in the outbreak - honest, hardworking businesses that formed the backbone of the community. Finding Gear Head Tools equipment in the post-outbreak world evokes nostalgia for simpler, more honest times.

## Implementation Guidelines

### In-Game Representation
- Gear Head Tools equipment appears in auto shops and garages
- Logo stickers on toolboxes and workbenches
- Promotional calendars on garage walls
- Tools can be found as collectible items

### Dialogue Integration
- Mechanics reference the brand with respect and nostalgia
- "That's a Gear Head wrench - they don't make 'em like that anymore"
- NPCs ask players if they've seen any Gear Head tools around
- Brand serves as small talk topic between automotive characters

### Environmental Storytelling
- Abandoned Gear Head factory tells story of interrupted normal life
- Tool catalogs in waiting rooms show pre-outbreak normalcy
- Rust and wear patterns on tools indicate heavy, reliable use
- Company calendar still shows date of outbreak
