# The Corner Cafe

A starter example location. Replace or adapt this for your world.

A weathered cafe on the corner of two busy streets. The owner, a retired baker, refuses to raise prices despite rising costs. The result is a gathering place for everyone from students to gang members who observe an unspoken truce inside its walls.

## Notable Features

- Cracked linoleum floor that's been there since the 1980s
- A back booth where deals have been made for decades
- The best coffee in the district at the lowest price

## Atmosphere

Warm, slightly chaotic, smelling of coffee and old newspapers. The kind of place that feels exactly the same no matter what time of day you visit.
