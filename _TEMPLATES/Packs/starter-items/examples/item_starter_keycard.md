# District Access Card

A starter example item. Replace or adapt this for your world.

These cards were issued by the previous administration and never properly revoked after the transition. Anyone who still has one either worked for the old regime or acquired it through less official means.

## Properties

- Grants access to controlled zones
- Holographic seal visible under UV light
- Expiry date: 3 years ago (still functional at most checkpoints)

## Narrative Use

The card is a conversation starter — anyone who sees it knows the holder has a story worth hearing. Or avoiding.
