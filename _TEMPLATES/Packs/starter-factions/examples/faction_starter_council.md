# The Merchants Council

A starter example faction. Replace or adapt this for your world.

The Merchants Council controls commercial licensing, import rights, and unofficial dispute resolution in the district. It presents itself as a neutral business association, but functions as the closest thing the district has to a governing body. Its neutrality is always conditional.

## Agenda

The Council's primary goal is stability — not justice, not equity, just the kind of order that keeps trade flowing. It will support any regime or faction that keeps the streets safe enough for commerce.

## Resources

- Commercial property across the district
- Relationships with every faction that needs to move goods
- Records of every business transaction for the past 70 years
