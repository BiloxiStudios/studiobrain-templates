# Cornerstone Diner

A starter example brand. Replace or adapt this for your world.

A family-owned diner that has outlasted three urban renewal programmes, two recessions, and one arson attempt. The sign has never been updated. The menu hasn't changed since 1987. That's the point.

## Brand Identity

Deliberately unglamorous. The Cornerstone trades on authenticity and community memory. In a neighbourhood that keeps losing its institutions, it's one of the last ones standing.

## Role in the World

The diner functions as neutral ground in the district — a place where factions eat on opposite sides of the same room. The owner enforces this informally and absolutely.
